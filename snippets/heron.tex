\begin{DoxyDocu}{heron}
\label{heron__k_8h_aed415be19d753a5bbc83160df70aff98_aed415be19d753a5bbc83160df70aff98}
use the Heron process to approximate {\itshape a} to the power of \code{1/k}

Or in other words this computes the $k^{th}$ root of {\itshape a}. As a special feature, if {\itshape k} is \code{-1} it computes the multiplicative inverse of {\itshape a}.


\begin{DoxyParams}{Parameters}
{\em a} & must be greater than \code{0.0} \\
\hline
{\em k} & should not be \code{0} and otherwise be between \code{DBL\_MIN\_EXP*FLT\_RDXRDX} and \code{DBL\_MAX\_EXP*FLT\_RDXRDX}.\\
\hline
\end{DoxyParams}
